# 🚀 pain-signal
**Power businesses with data-driven insights into customer pain points.** pain-signal is a tool that identifies and validates customer pain points, reducing time and resources spent on validating business ideas.

## Why pain-signal?
* **Data-driven**: Leverages sales calls and customer interaction data for actionable insights.
* **Time-saving**: Reduces time spent on validating business ideas.
* **Resource-efficient**: Increases the effectiveness of resources spent on business idea validation.
* **Actionable insights**: Provides concrete data to inform business decisions.
* **Integration-ready**: Designed for seamless integration with existing sales and customer interaction systems.
* **Scalable**: Built to handle large volumes of customer interaction data.
* **Flexible**: Adaptable to various business needs and use cases.

## Feature Overview
| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Pain Point Identification | Identifies customer pain points from sales calls and customer interaction data |
| Data Integration | Integrates with existing sales and customer interaction systems |
| Insight Generation | Generates actionable insights from customer pain point data |
| Validation | Validates business ideas based on customer pain point data |
